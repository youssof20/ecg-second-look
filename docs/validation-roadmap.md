# Validation roadmap

Synthetic teaching demos and local benchmarks do not establish clinical validity. Moving beyond a prototype would require, at minimum:

1. Technical validation against paired ECG images and digital waveforms.
2. Robustness testing across devices, paper formats, lighting, hospitals, and camera quality.
3. Retrospective evaluation on de-identified multi-site data with predefined endpoints.
4. Clinician usability testing in realistic time pressure.
5. Subgroup and failure-mode analysis, including systematic refusals.
6. Prospective silent evaluation without affecting care.
7. Regulatory and institutional review appropriate to the jurisdiction.
8. Monitored deployment only after evidence and authorization — not after a larger model or longer disclaimer list.

An open-source maintainer cannot skip these stages by adding disclaimers alone.
